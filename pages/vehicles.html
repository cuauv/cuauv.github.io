Two Brothers Castor & Pollux

CUAUV pioneered the AUVSI competition in 2016 by building not one but two subs, Thor and Loki. We continue this proud tradition with our brand new vehicles Castor and Pollux. Castor and Pollux were demigods and the patron saints of sailors. With CUAUV, Castor and Pollux will return to the seas at AUVSI summer 2018.
