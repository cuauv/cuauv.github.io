Outreach

Sharing our passions with the community.

Boy and Girl Scouts

AUV invites local boy scouts to campus and exposes them to the excitement of engineering. We teach programming and robotics, which helps them explore potential paths into STEM. This is the capstone project of our outreach initiatives.

Engineering Diversity Hosting Weekend

We showcase our labs and vehicles to prospective engineering students from underrepresented backgrounds, both to encourage them to come to Cornell and to learn from our project team opportunities.

BOOM

BOOM (Bits on our Minds) is a showcase hosted by Cornell's Computer and Information Science (CIS) department. We showcase our work alongside other project teams, CIS research, and independent projects to help the Cornell community and the public understand the potential and function of AUV. We've won BOOM's People's Choice Award multiple times!

Raw Expo

Along with other project teams and research groups, we demonstrate the messy and non-linear process of building underwater autonomous vehicles. The intent of this Expo is to expose non-engineering students to the creative processes in engineering.
