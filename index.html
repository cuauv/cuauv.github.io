Introducing

Castor & Pollux

2 New Vehicles

CUAUV’s autonomous underwater vehicles are a testament to our team’s engineering excellence. Each year we build upon our past vehicles and develop two entirely new AUVs to simultaneously complete the AUVSI RoboSub course.

7 Championships

As a seven-time RoboSub champion, CUAUV embodies a tradition of excellence and continuously pushes the envelope of autonomous underwater vehicle technology.

Diverse Team

CUAUV is entirely undergraduate-run, consisting of over 40 students spanning 8 diverse academic disciplines. We are divided into 4 subteams: Software, Mechanical, Electrical, and Business.

Outreach

Description

Along with hosting lab tours with visiting companies, we organize multiple outreach events to better acquaint the greater Cornell and Ithaca communities with our team and our sponsors. Along with lab tours to visiting schools and organizations, we visit local elementary, middle and high schools for various STEM events.
