Come join CUAUV in a celebration of the team's 10th Anniversary! On Sunday, November 23 from 5:30-7:30pm at Teagle Pool, CUAUV will be conducting an open pool test. You will have the opportunity to see Triton in action, view slideshows depicting the team's history, and meet members of the 2008-2009 team. Feel free to stop by or stay for the entire time. Light refreshments will be served.
