--- layout: post title: What Difference Can One Day Make? submitter: prteam date: 2015-03-25 13:01:50 old_id: 273 --- What difference can one day make? Cornell Giving Day is a 24-hour coming-together to support Cornell. You can make a gift to CUAUV by visiting https://givingday.cornell.edu/, selecting College of Engineering under "College or area", selecting Other under "Fund", and entering "CUAUV" under Comments/Other Designation. Your gift can help unlock up to $300,000 in challenge money to Cornell colleges, schools, and units! Let's make a difference!
