On Sunday, November 23, CUAUV celebrated its 10th Anniversary with an Open Pool Test. Faculty advisors, support staff, the Cornell Community and the Ithaca Community were all invited to attend. The event was a success with the team demonstrating the capabilities of its 2008 competition vehicle. For more pictures from the event visit the Image Galleries.
