CUAUV participated in the on campus event Bits on Our Minds (BOOM). BOOM is an annual research conference that showcases student efforts and creativity in digital technology and applications. At the event the CUAUV team displayed parts of their new vehicle. Team members were also on hand to describe the vehicle and talk about the team. In addition, there was a demonstration running where BOOM visitors could see first-hand how the vehicles vision system works. Visitors also had the opportunity to friend last years competition vehicle, Proteus T. Automaton, on Facebook. The event was held on February 27th in Duffield Atrium. Participation was open to all Cornell students, and community members.
