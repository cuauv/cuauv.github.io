--- layout: page title: Nova Completes First Autonomous Run at Transdec submitter: prteam date: 2009-07-29 16:18:58 old_id: 81 ---

This morning, Nova had its first autonomous run at TRANSDEC. This first test was very successful. The vehicle passed through the validation gate, rammed the buoy, followed a pipeline, went under the barbed wire, grabbed the briefcase, and surfaced in the recovery zone. Continual updates will be posted here for the duration of the competition.
