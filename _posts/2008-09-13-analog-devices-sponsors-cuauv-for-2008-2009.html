--- layout: post title: Analog Devices Sponsors CUAUV for 2008-2009 submitter: prteam date: 2008-09-13 03:14:44 old_id: 51 ---

CUAUV is pleased to announce that Analog Devices will continue to sponsor the team for 2008-2009. Analog Devices has generously donated two copies of Analog's VisualDSP++ for Sharcs. These licenses will allow the team to switch over to Sharc processors, which will form basis of a new USBL direction-finding system for the new competition vehicle.
